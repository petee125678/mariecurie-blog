Hello,

As you saw in my past writing from when I was a teen girl, my time mechanism (I have heard they would now call it a “machine”) worked! I could retrieve the lost “machine” because I forgot where I left it. Recently(about 2 days ago), I found the machine again, and I have since decided that I will be attempting to go on a vacation in the future! I have found that my money is worth more since I am from the past. I have about 45 francs that I am willing to use for my travel, which, according to my calculations, is about 20,000 “euros” in 2026! That will give me plenty of money for my vacation, and I hope I can go to many countries.
