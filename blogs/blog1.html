Hello!

The time mechanism that I built successfully worked! I was able to advance further to the year 2026, and I met a class of children my age; they were all as respectful as monks! I was able to practice French with them too. I originally tried to go to the year 2104 because when you add the entire sum of my name, you get 104! But sadly, it was a random surprise when my mechanism didn't work properly due to a small speck of dust, smaller than a grain of rice, that went in between the fragile wires and made them malfunction. We all went to a camp where I got to have lots of fun with them, but I didn't bring any of my clothes, so some of the girls lent me some of their own, their garments; they felt like silk! We did many different activities, and they taught me about everything in their time, but in the end I had to go back to my time.
